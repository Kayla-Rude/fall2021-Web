General Info

My name is Kayla Rude and I am currently a senior in the computer science department at CU Boulder. I am finally back in Boulder after spending the past year and a half back in my hometown in northern Illinois. I grew up in the Chicagoland area and perpetually remain on the hunt for decent pizza in Boulder (no Cosmos is not decent pizza). I have a variety of hobbies all of which tap into my creative side. I am passionate about mental health and disability advocacy. I am an avid photographer and am an LA for the image class within ATLAS. I think there is something incredibly powerful about crafting an image or piece of content that shares your unique perspective of the world. Finally the hobby that I am most passionate about is nail art. Over the course of the pandemic doing nail art and sharing it with my Instagram community has helped me feel connected.

Goals for the class

My goal for this class is to gain a better understanding of front end web development. I just finished software development and methods so I have an advanced knowledge of database systems and learned a lot of HTML, javascript, and CSS in that class. My passion lies in creating accessible technology. I want to work on front end development and creating interfaces accessible to everyone. I hope that this class will touch on some accesibility content and pushes students to consider how their designes will be received by the disabled community.
